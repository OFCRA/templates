# Sitac & Weather

Following the American assault on Pumado Base in the north of the island. The command of the Russian forces decided to launch an offensive on the town of Kinsella. The US embassy in the city center is a prime target for destabilizing Swazili region. A high ranking officer is a post west of the city in a well-protected military complex. His elimination could force the US military to withdraw from the city.

Mission start time: 16h00 loc.  
Duration: 2h  
Fog: none 
Ceiling: medium  
Cloud cover: 4/8  
Forecast: stable
